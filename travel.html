Copenhagen, Sweden

Copenhagen is the capital and most populous city of Denmark. Danes rank among the world's most happy people. Copenhagen is famous for royal history, modern architecture and loads of culture with sustainable living and a mouthwatering restaurant scene.

Vrachos, Greece

Vrachos is a very beautiful and tropical place in Greece. This place is located on the second leg in Greece. The beach in this place is sandy with small pebbles in various colors and the sea is clean and has a beautiful bright blue color.

Kopaonik, Serbia

Kopaonik is one of the largest mountain ranges in Serbia. It is located in the central part of the country with a small part in Northern Kosovo. The highest point is Pančićev Vrv (2,017 m). This place is suitable for skiing and hiking. Kopaonik is a place with beautiful nature and clean air.
