In my free time I love to cook. I really love preparing healthy and delicious meals for the people I love. My favorite dishes are chocolate protein oatmeal with forest fruits and protein crisp wrap. My favorite places in Skopje where they prepare healthy and fresh food are: Ljubovna Kukica, Zdrava Stanica, Fitness House and Healthyish. My friend Anika and I have our own blog on Instagram where we post healthy food recipes called cooking_with_anika_and_mila.
